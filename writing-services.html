About

Welcome to Ludus Integritas LLC, the home base and parent company to several ventures. We offer sales and services to reflect the needs of those interested in self-defense and life emergencies. Whether it’s firearms or accessories for sale, training in how to make holes or patch them, or writing about all the above, Ludus Integritas is a here to serve. The name Ludus Integritas comes from the Roman for school (ludus) and Integritas reflects wholeness or completeness, describing a holistic approach to training.
Also from the Romans comes the phrase “Libens, Volens, Potens!” This was shouted by soldiers preparing to enter battle and it indicated that they were Ready, Willing, and Able to do their job. This concept addressed their overall status both mentally and physically.

Who we are

Sean Curtis is a law enforcement officer of 20 years who has served in patrol, investigations, and SWAT. During his career Curtis was an Emergency Medical Technician and Type II firefighter (wildland), also serving on an incident management team as a Type III Incident Commander. Curtis was a coordinator for search and rescue efforts in one of the busiest (total searches/rescues) mountain counties in Colorado. He is currently an American Red Cross instructor for First Aid/CPR/AED and a Colorado POST certified firearms instructor as well an A.L.I.C.E instructor.
Curtis is also a nationally published writer/photographer with articles appearing in PoliceOne.com, Military1.com, and Gun World Magazine. His works include gun and gear reviews as well as features on skillsets in tactical settings such as man-tracking, emergency medicine, and avalanche survival.
Summer Curtis is a former Emergency Medical Technician and Certified Nursing Assistant.  She is an authorized American Red Cross instructor for First Aid/CPR/AED, Babysitting Training, Wilderness Survival, and Preparedness Education. Summer strives to create a fun learning environment for memorable lifelong learning.
